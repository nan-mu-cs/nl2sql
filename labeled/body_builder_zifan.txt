1. How many body builders are there?

3. List the snatch score and clean jerk score of body builders in ascending order of snatch score .

SELECT Snatch, Clean_Jerk
FROM body_builder
order by Snatch ASC

SELECT avg(Snatch)
FROM body_builder

5. What is the clean and jerk score of the body builder with the highest total score.

SELECT Clean_Jerk
FROM body_builder

SELECT T2.Name
FROM body_builder as T1 join people as T2

SELECT T2.Name
FROM body_builder as T1 join people as T2 
ON T1.People_ID = T2.People_ID

FROM body_builder as T1 join people as T2
