1. How many farms are there?

SELECT count(*)
FROM farm

2. List the total number of horses of farms in ascending order.

SELECT Total_Horses
FROM farm
order by Total_Horses ASC

3. What are the hosts of competitions whose theme is not "Aliens"?

SELECT Hosts
FROM farm_competition
where Theme != 'Aliens'

4. Who are the themes of farm competitions sorted by year in ascending order?

SELECT Theme
FROM farm_competition
order by Year ASC

5. What are the average number of working horses of farms with more than 5000 total number of horses?

SELECT avg(Working_Horses)
FROM farm
WHERE Total_Horses > 5000

6. What are the maximum and minimum number of cows of all farms.

SELECT max(Cows), min(Cows)
FROM farm

7. How many different status do cities have?

SELECT count(distinct Status)
FROM city

8. List official names of cities in descending order of population.

SELECT Official_Name
FROM city
order by Population DESC

9. List the official name and status of the city with the largest population

SELECT Official_Name, Status
FROM city
order by Population DESC limit 1

10. Show the years and the official names of the host cities of competitions.

SELECT T2.Year, T1.Official_Name
FROM city as T1 join farm_competition as T2 
ON T1.City_ID = T2.Host_city_ID

11. Show the official names of the cities that have host more than one competition.

SELECT T1.Official_Name
FROM city as T1 join farm_competition as T2 
ON T1.City_ID = T2.Host_city_ID
group by T2.Host_city_ID
Having COUNT(*) > 1

12. Show the status of the city that have host the most number of competitions.

SELECT T1.Status
FROM city as T1 join farm_competition as T2 
ON T1.City_ID = T2.Host_city_ID
group by T2.Host_city_ID
order by COUNT(*) DESC limit 1

13. Please show the themes of competitions with host cities having population larger than 1000.

SELECT T2.Theme
FROM city as T1 join farm_competition as T2 
ON T1.City_ID = T2.Host_city_ID
Where T1.Population > 1000
