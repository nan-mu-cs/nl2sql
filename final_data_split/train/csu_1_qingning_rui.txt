6. Find the name of the campuses that is in Northridge, Los Angeles or in San Francisco, San Francisco.
P: What campuses are located in Northridge, Los Angeles or in San Francisco, San Francisco?

union
select campus from campuses where location = "San Francisco" and county = "San Francisco"

Or, alternatively:

select campus
from campuses

14. Which discipline has the most enrollments? Show the name.
P: What is the name of the discipline with the most enrollments?

select T1.name
from disciplines as T1 join discipline_enrollments as T2
on T2.discipline = T1.id
group by T1.id
order by count(*) desc
limit 1

15. What is the name and number of full-time students of the campus that has the most "Computer and Info. Sciences" students enrolled in year 2004.
P: What is the campus name and FTE_AY of full-time students who go to the campus with the most "Computer and Info. Science" students enrolled in 2004? 

select T1.campus, T2.FTE_AY
from campuses as T1 join enrollments as T2
on T1.id = T2.campus
join discipline_enrollments as T3
on T3.campus = T1.id
join disciplines as T4
on T3.discipline = T4.id
where t3.year = 2004 and T4.name = "Computer and Info. Sciences"
group by T3.campus
order by count(*) desc
limit 1

20. For each campus in Los Angeles or Orange counties report the total number of degrees granted between 1998 and 2002.
P: For each campus located in LA or Orange county, how many degrees were conferred between 1998 and 2002?
select T1.campus, sum(T2.degrees)
from campuses as T1 join degrees as T2
on T1.id = T2.campus
where (T1.county = "Los Angeles" or T1.county = "Orange") and T2.year >= 1998 and T2.year <= 2002
group by T1.campus
