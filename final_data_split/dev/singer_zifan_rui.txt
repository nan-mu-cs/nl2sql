1. How many singers are there?
P: What is the count of singers?

SELECT count(*)
FROM singer

2. List the name of singers in ascending order of net worth.
P: What are the names of singers ordered by ascending net worth?

SELECT Name
FROM singer
order by Net_Worth_Millions ASC

3. What are the birth year and citizenship of singers?
P: What are the birth years and citizenships of the singers?

SELECT Birth_Year, Citizenship
FROM singer

4. List the name of singers whose citizenship is not "France".
P: What are the names of the singers who are not French citizens?

SELECT Name
FROM singer
Where Citizenship != "France"

5. Show the name of singers whose birth year is either 1948 or 1949?
P: What are the names of the singers whose birth years are either 1948 or 1949?

SELECT Name
FROM singer

6. What is the name of the singer with the largest net worth?
P: What is the name of the singer who is worth the most?

P: For each citizenship, how many singers are from that country?

10. Show titles of songs and names of singers.
P: What are the song titles and singer names?

SELECT T2.Title, T1.Name
FROM singer as T1 join song as T2 
ON T1.Singer_ID = T2.Singer_ID

11. Show distinct names of singers that have songs with sales more than 300000.
P: what are the different names of the singers that have sales more than 300000?

SELECT distinct T1.Name
FROM singer as T1 join song as T2 
ON T1.Singer_ID = T2.Singer_ID
Where T2.Sales > 300000

12. Show the names of singers that have more than one song.
P: What are the names of the singers that have more than one songs?

SELECT T1.Name
FROM singer as T1 join song as T2 
ON T1.Singer_ID = T2.Singer_ID
group by T1.Name
Having COUNT(*) > 1

13. Show the names of singers and the total sales of their songs.
P: For each singer name, what is the total sales for their songs?

SELECT T1.Name, sum(T2.Sales)
FROM singer as T1 join song as T2 
ON T1.Singer_ID = T2.Singer_ID
group by T1.Name
