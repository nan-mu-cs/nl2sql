1. How many teachers are there?
P: What is the total count of teachers?

SELECT count(*)
FROM teacher

2. List the names of teachers in ascending order of age.
P: What are the names of the teachers ordered by ascending age?

SELECT Name
FROM teacher
order by Age ASC

3. What are the age and hometown of teachers?
P: What is the age and hometown of every teacher?

SELECT Age, Hometown
FROM teacher

4. List the name of teachers whose hometown is not "Little Lever Urban District".
P: What are the names of the teachers whose hometown is not "Little Lever Urban District"?

SELECT Name
FROM teacher
Where Hometown != "Little Lever Urban Distric"

5. Show the name of teachers aged either 32 or 33?
P: What are the names of the teachers who are aged either 32 or 33?

SELECT Name
FROM teacher
Where Age = 32 or Age = 33

6. What is the hometown of the youngest teacher?
P: Where is the youngest teacher from?

SELECT Hometown
FROM teacher
order by Age ASC limit 1

7. Show different hometown of teachers and the number of teachers from each hometown.
P: For each hometown, how many teachers are there?

SELECT Hometown, COUNT(*)
FROM teacher
group by Hometown
 
8. List the most common hometown of teachers.
P: What is the most commmon hometowns for teachers?

SELECT Hometown
FROM teacher
group by Hometown
order by COUNT(*) DESC limit 1

9. Show the hometowns shared by at least two teachers.
P: What are the towns from which at least two teachers come from?

SELECT Hometown
FROM teacher
group by Hometown
Having COUNT(*) >= 2

10. Show names of teachers and the courses they are arranged to teach.
P: What is the name of each teacher and what course they teach?

SELECT T3.Name, T2.Course
FROM course_arrange as T1 join course as T2 
ON T1.Course_ID = T2.Course_ID join teacher as T3
on T1.Teacher_ID = T3.Teacher_ID

11. Show names of teachers and the courses they are arranged to teach in ascending alphabetical order of the teacher's name.
P: What are the names of the teachers and the courses they teach in ascending alphabetical order by the name of the teacher?

SELECT T3.Name, T2.Course
FROM course_arrange as T1 join course as T2 
ON T1.Course_ID = T2.Course_ID join teacher as T3
on T1.Teacher_ID = T3.Teacher_ID
order by T3.Name

12. Show the name of the teacher for the math course.
P: What are the names of the people who teach math courses?

SELECT T3.Name
FROM course_arrange as T1 join course as T2 
ON T1.Course_ID = T2.Course_ID join teacher as T3
on T1.Teacher_ID = T3.Teacher_ID
Where T2.Course = "Math"

13. Show names of teachers and the number of courses they teach.
P: What are the names of the teachers and how many courses do they teach?

SELECT T2.Name, COUNT(*)
FROM course_arrange as T1 join teacher as T2
on T1.Teacher_ID = T2.Teacher_ID
group by T2.Name

14. Show names of teachers that teach at least two courses.
P: What are the names of the teachers who teach at least two courses?

SELECT T2.Name
FROM course_arrange as T1 join teacher as T2
on T1.Teacher_ID = T2.Teacher_ID
group by T2.Name
Having COUNT(*) >= 2
