5.List the forename and surname of all distinct drivers who once had laptime less than 93000 milliseconds?
P: What are the forenames and surnames of all unique drivers who had a lap time of less than 93000 milliseconds?

SELECT distinct T1.forename, T1.surname
FROM drivers as T1 join laptimes as T2
on T1.driverid=T2.driverid
WHERE T2.milliseconds<93000

from drivers as T1 join pitstops as T2
on T1.driverid = T2.driverid
group by T1.driverid
having count(*) > 8
