1. How many technicians are there?
P: What is the number of technicians?

SELECT count(*)
FROM technician

2. List the names of technicians in ascending order of age.
P: What are the names of the technicians by ascending order of age?

SELECT Name
FROM technician
order by Age ASC

3. What are the team and starting year of technicians?
P: What is the team and starting year for each technician?

SELECT Team, Starting_Year
FROM technician

4. List the name of technicians whose team is not "NYY".
P: What is the name of the technician whose team is not 'NYY'?

SELECT Name
FROM technician
Where Team != "NYY"

5. Show the name of technicians aged either 36 or 37
P: What are the names of the technicians aged either 36 or 37?

SELECT Name
FROM technician
Where Age = 36 or Age = 37

6. What is the starting year of the oldest technicians?
P: What is the starting year for the oldest technician?

SELECT Starting_Year
FROM technician
order by Age DESC limit 1

7. Show different teams of technicians and the number of technicians in each team.
P: For each team, how many technicians are there?

SELECT Team, COUNT(*)
FROM technician
group by Team
 
8. Please show the team that has the most number of technicians.
P: What are the teams with the most technicians?

SELECT Team
FROM technician

9. Show the team that have at least two technicians.
P: What is the team with at least 2 technicians?

SELECT Team
FROM technician
group by Team
Having COUNT(*) >= 2

10. Show names of technicians and series of machines they are assigned to repair.
P: What are the names of technicians and the machine series that they repair?

SELECT T3.Name, T2.Machine_series
FROM repair_assignment as T1 join machine as T2 
ON T1.machine_id = T2.machine_id join technician as T3
on T1.technician_ID = T3.technician_ID

11. Show names of technicians in ascending order of quality rank of the machine they are assigned.
P: What are the names of the technicians by ascending order of quality rank for the machine they are assigned?

SELECT T3.Name
FROM repair_assignment as T1 join machine as T2 
ON T1.machine_id = T2.machine_id join technician as T3
on T1.technician_ID = T3.technician_ID
order by T2.quality_rank

12. Show names of technicians who are assigned to repair machines with value point more than 70. 
P: What are the names of the technicians that are assigned to repair machines with more point values than 70?

SELECT T3.Name
FROM repair_assignment as T1 join machine as T2 
ON T1.machine_id = T2.machine_id join technician as T3
on T1.technician_ID = T3.technician_ID
Where T2.value_points > 70

13. Show names of technicians and the number of machines they are assigned to repair.
P: What are the names of the technicians  and how many machines are they assigned to repair?

SELECT T2.Name, COUNT(*)
FROM repair_assignment as T1 join technician as T2
on T1.technician_ID = T2.technician_ID
group by T2.Name

14. List the names of technicians who have not been assigned to repair machines.
P: What are the names of the technicians that have not been assigned to repair machines?

SELECT Name
from technician
where technician_id not in (SELECT technician_id FROM repair_assignment)
