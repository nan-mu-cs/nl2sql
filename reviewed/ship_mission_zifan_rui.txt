1. How many ships are there?
P: What is the number of ships?

SELECT count(*)
FROM ship

2. List the name of ships in ascending order of tonnage.
P: what are the names of the ships ordered by ascending tonnage?

SELECT Name
FROM ship
order by Tonnage ASC

3. What are the type and nationality of ships?
P: What are the types and nationalities of every ship?

SELECT Type, Nationality
FROM ship

4. List the name of ships whose nationality is not "United States".
P: What are the names of the ships that are not from the United States?

SELECT Name
FROM ship
Where Nationality != "United States"

5. Show the name of ships whose nationality is either United States or United Kingdom.
P: What are the names of the ships  that are from either the US or the UK?

SELECT Name
FROM ship
Where Nationality = "United States" or Nationality = "United Kingdom"

6. What is the name of the ship with the largest tonnage?
P: What is the ship with the largest amount of tonnage called?

SELECT Name
FROM ship
order by Tonnage DESC limit 1

7. Show different types of ships and the number of ships of each type.
P: For each type, how many ships are there?

SELECT Type, COUNT(*)
FROM ship
group by Type
 
8. Please show the most common type of ships.
P: What is the most common type of ships?

SELECT Type
FROM ship
group by Type
order by COUNT(*) DESC limit 1

9. List the nations that have more than two ships.
P: What are the nations that have more than two ships?

SELECT Nationality
FROM ship
group by Nationality
Having COUNT(*) > 2

10. Show different types of ships and the average tonnage of ships of each type.
P: For each type, what is the average tonnage?

SELECT Type, avg(Tonnage)
FROM ship
group by Type

11. Show codes and fates of missions, and names of ships involved.
P: What are the mission codes, fates, and names of the ships involved?

SELECT T1.Code, T1.Fate, T2.Name
FROM mission as T1 join ship as T2 
ON T1.Ship_ID = T2.Ship_ID

12. Show names of ships involved in a mission launched after 1928.
P: What are the names of ships that were involved in a mission launched after 1928?

SELECT T2.Name
FROM mission as T1 join ship as T2 
ON T1.Ship_ID = T2.Ship_ID
Where T1.Launched_Year > 1928

13. Show the distinct fate of missions that involve ships with nationality "United States"
P: What are the different fates of the mission that involved ships from the United States?

SELECT distinct T1.Fate
FROM mission as T1 join ship as T2 
ON T1.Ship_ID = T2.Ship_ID
Where T2.Nationality = "United States"

14. List the name of ships that are not involved in any mission
P: What are the names of the ships that are not involved in any missions?

SELECT Name
from ship
where Ship_ID not in (SELECT Ship_ID FROM mission)
