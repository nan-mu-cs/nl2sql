10. Show teams of players and the locations of schools they go to.

SELECT T1.Team, T2.Location
FROM player as T1 join school as T2 
ON T1.School_ID = T2.School_ID

11. Show location of schools that have more than 1 player.

SELECT T2.Location
FROM player as T1 join school as T2 
ON T1.School_ID = T2.School_ID
group by T1.School_ID
Having COUNT(*) > 1

12. Show the denomination of the school that have the most number of players.

SELECT T2.Denomination
FROM player as T1 join school as T2 
ON T1.School_ID = T2.School_ID
group by T1.School_ID
Having COUNT(*) > 1
order by COUNT(*) DESC limit 1
