1. How many products are there?

SELECT count(*)
FROM Products

2. List the name of products in ascending order of price.

SELECT Product_Name
FROM Products
order by Product_Price ASC

3. What are the names and type codes of products?

SELECT Product_Name, Product_Type_Code
FROM Products

4. Show the prices of the products named "Dining" or "Trading Policy"

SELECT Product_Price
FROM Products
Where Product_Name = "Dining" or Product_Name = "Trading Policy"

5. What is the average price for products?

SELECT avg(Product_Price)
FROM Products

6. What is the name of the product with the highest price?

SELECT Product_Name
FROM Products
order by Product_Price DESC limit 1

7. Show different type codes of products and the number of products with each type code.

SELECT Product_Type_Code, COUNT(*)
FROM Products
group by Product_Type_Code
 
8. Show the most common type code of products.

SELECT Product_Type_Code
FROM Products
group by Product_Type_Code
order by COUNT(*) DESC limit 1

9. Show the product type codes that have at least two products.

SELECT Product_Type_Code
FROM Products
group by Product_Type_Code
Having COUNT(*) >= 2

10. Show the product type codes that have both products with price higher than 4500 and products with price lower than 3000.

SELECT Product_Type_Code
from Products
Where Product_Price > 4500
Intersect 
SELECT Product_Type_Code
from Products
Where Product_Price < 3000

11. Show the names of products and the number of events they are in.

SELECT T1.Product_Name, COUNT(*)
FROM Products as T1 join Products_in_Events as T2
on T1.Product_ID = T2.Product_ID
group by T1.Product_Name

12. Show the names of products and the number of events they are in sorted by the number of events in descending order.

SELECT T1.Product_Name, COUNT(*)
FROM Products as T1 join Products_in_Events as T2
on T1.Product_ID = T2.Product_ID
group by T1.Product_Name
order by COUNT(*) DESC

13. Show the names of products that are in at least two events.

SELECT T1.Product_Name
FROM Products as T1 join Products_in_Events as T2
on T1.Product_ID = T2.Product_ID
group by T1.Product_Name
Having COUNT(*) >= 2

14. Show the names of products that are in at least two events in ascending alphabetical order of product name.

SELECT T1.Product_Name
FROM Products as T1 join Products_in_Events as T2
on T1.Product_ID = T2.Product_ID
group by T1.Product_Name
Having COUNT(*) >= 2
order by T1.Product_Name

15. List the names of products that are not in any event.

SELECT Product_Name
from Products
where Product_ID not in (SELECT Product_ID FROM Products_in_Events)
