8. Which discipline has the most enrollments? Show the name.

select T1.name
from disciplines as T1 join "discipline-enrollments" as T2
on T2.discipline = T1.id
group by T1.id
order by count(*) desc
limit 1

9. What is the name and number of full-time students of the campus that has the most "Computer and Info. Sciences" students enrolled in year 2004.

select T1.campus, T2.FTE_AY
from campuses as T1 join enrollments as T2
on T1.id = T2.campus
join "discipline-enrollments" as T3
on T3.campus = T1.id
join disciplines as T4
on T3.discipline = T4.id
where t3.year = 2004 and T4.name = "Computer and Info. Sciences"
group by T3.campus
order by count(*) desc
limit 1
