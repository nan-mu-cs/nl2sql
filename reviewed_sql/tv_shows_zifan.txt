1. How many radios are there?

SELECT count(*)
FROM radio

2. List the transmitters of radios in ascending order of ERP.

SELECT Transmitter
FROM radio
order by ERP_kW ASC

3. What are the names and original air dates of tv shows?

SELECT tv_show_name, Original_Airdate
FROM tv_show

4. List the station names of city channels whose affiliation is not "ABC".

SELECT Station_name
FROM city_channel
Where Affiliation != "ABC"

5. Show the transmitters of radios whose ERP is bigger than 150 or smaller than 50.

SELECT Transmitter
from radio
Where ERP_kW > 150 or ERP_kW < 30

6. What is the transmitter of the radio with the largest ERP_kW?

SELECT Transmitter
FROM radio
order by ERP_kW DESC limit 1

7. What is the ERP of radios?

SELECT avg(ERP_kW)
FROM radio

8. Show different affiliations of city channels and the number of city channels with each affiliation.

SELECT Affiliation, COUNT(*)
FROM city_channel
group by Affiliation
 
9. Please show the most common affiliation of city channels

SELECT Affiliation
FROM city_channel
group by Affiliation
order by COUNT(*) DESC limit 1

10. List the affiliations shared by more than three city channels.

SELECT Affiliation
FROM city_channel
group by Affiliation
Having COUNT(*) > 3

11. Show the cities and station names of city channels in ascending alphabetical order of station name.

SELECT City, Station_name
FROM city_channel
order by Station_name ASC

12. Show the transmitters of radios and the cities of the channels they are associated with.

SELECT T3.Transmitter, T2.City
FROM city_channel_radio as T1 join city_channel as T2 
ON T1.City_channel_ID = T2.ID join radio as T3
on T1.Radio_ID = T3.Radio_ID

13. Show transmitters of radios and the station names of the channels they are associated with in descending order of the ERP of the radios.

SELECT T3.Transmitter, T2.Station_name
FROM city_channel_radio as T1 join city_channel as T2 
ON T1.City_channel_ID = T2.ID join radio as T3
on T1.Radio_ID = T3.Radio_ID
order by T3.ERP_kW

14. Show the transmitters of the radios and the number of city channels they are associated with.

SELECT T3.Transmitter, COUNT(*)
FROM city_channel_radio as T1 join city_channel as T2 
ON T1.City_channel_ID = T2.ID join radio as T3
on T1.Radio_ID = T3.Radio_ID
group by T3.Transmitter

15. Show distinct transmitters of radios that are not associated with any city channel.

SELECT Transmitter
FROM radio
Where Radio_ID not in (SELECT Radio_ID FROM city_channel_radio)
