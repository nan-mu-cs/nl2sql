2. Show the season, the player, and the name of the country that player belongs to.

select T2.Season, T2.Player, T1.Country_name
from country as T1 JOIN match_season as T2 
on T1.Country_id = T2.Country

Which players are from Indonesia?

select T2.Player
from country as T1 JOIN match_season as T2 
on T1.Country_id = T2.Country
where T1.Country_name = "Indonesia"

What are the distinct positions of the players from a country whose capital is Dublin?

select distinct T2.Position
from country as T1 JOIN match_season as T2 
on T1.Country_id = T2.Country
where T1.Capital = "Dublin"
