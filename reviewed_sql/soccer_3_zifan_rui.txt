1. How many clubs are there?

FROM club

2. List the name of clubs in ascending alphabetical order.

SELECT Name
FROM club
order by Name ASC

3. What are the managers and captains of clubs?

SELECT Manager, Captain
FROM club

4. List the name of clubs whose manufacturer is not "Nike"

SELECT Name
FROM club
Where "Manufacturer" != "Nike"

5. What are the names of players in ascending order of wins count?

SELECT Name
FROM player
order by Wins_count ASC

6. What is the name of the player with the highest earnings?

SELECT Name
FROM player
order by Earnings DESC limit 1

7. What are the distinct countries of players with earnings higher than 1200000?

SELECT distinct Country
FROM player
Where Earnings > 1200000

8. What is the country of the player with the highest earnings among players that have more than 2 win counts?

11. Show names of players from the club with manager "Sam Allardyce".

FROM club as T1 join player as T2 
ON T1.Club_ID = T2.Club_ID

12. Show names of clubs in descending order of average earnings of players belonging.

SELECT T1.Name
FROM club as T1 join player as T2 
ON T1.Club_ID = T2.Club_ID
group by T1.Club_ID
order by avg(T2.Earnings) DESC
