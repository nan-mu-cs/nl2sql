1.List the title of all cartoon.

FROM Cartoon;

2. List all cartoon directed by "Ben Jones".
SELECT Title
FROM Cartoon
WHERE Directed_by="Ben Jones";

3. How many cartoons were written by "Joseph Kuhr"?
SELECT count(*)
FROM Cartoon
WHERE Written_by="Joseph Kuhr";

4. What is the original air date of cartoon with title "The Rise of the Blue Beetle!"?

5. List the title of all cartoon directed by "Ben Jones" or "Brandon Vietti".
SELECT Title
FROM Cartoon

6. Which country has the most of TV Channels? List the country and number of TV Channels it has.
SELECT Country, count(*)
FROM TV_Channel
GROUP BY Country
ORDER BY count(*) DESC
LIMIT 1;

10. How many TV Channel using language English?
SELECT count(*)

11. List the language used least number of TV Channel. List language and number of TV Channel.
SELECT Language, count(*)
FROM TV_Channel
GROUP BY Language
ORDER BY count(*) asc
LIMIT 1;

12. What is the TV Channel shows cartoon "The Rise of the Blue Beetle!"? List the TV Channel's series name.
SELECT T1.series_name
FROM TV_Channel AS T1 JOIN Cartoon AS T2 ON T1.id=T2.Channel
WHERE T2.Title="The Rise of the Blue Beetle!";
